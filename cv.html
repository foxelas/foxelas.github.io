eleni aloupogianni

information engineer

Eleni (Elena) Aloupogianni received her B.Sc.&M.Sc. diploma from National Technical University of Athens, Greece, in 2016. She earned a M.Eng. degree from the Department of Information and Communications Engineering, Tokyo Institute of Technology (TokyoTech), Japan. At present, she is enrolled in the Human Centered Science and Biomedical Engineering doctoral course of TokyoTech and works on gross pathology image processing and multi-spectral imaging. She is with Obi Laboratory, Institute of Innovative Research (IIR) in TokoyoTech.
