information engineer

Eleni (Elena) Aloupogianni received her B.S.& M.S. diploma in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the National Technical University of Athens, Greece, in 2016. She earned a M.E. in Information and Communications Engineering in 2019 and a Ph.D. in Human-Centered Science and Biomedical Engineering in 2022, from Tokyo Institute of Technology, Japan. Her research interests include image processing, computer-assisted diagnosis, hyperspectral imaging and statistical analysis.
